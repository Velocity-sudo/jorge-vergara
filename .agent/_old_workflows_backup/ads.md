# Ads — Paid Advertising Audit & Optimization

Use these slash commands to trigger advertising analysis:

## Available Commands

### `/ads audit`
Full multi-platform audit. Read `.agent/skills/ads-audit/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads google`
Google Ads deep analysis. Read `.agent/skills/ads-google/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads meta`
Meta Ads deep analysis. Read `.agent/skills/ads-meta/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads youtube`
YouTube Ads analysis. Read `.agent/skills/ads-youtube/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads linkedin`
LinkedIn Ads deep analysis. Read `.agent/skills/ads-linkedin/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads tiktok`
TikTok Ads deep analysis. Read `.agent/skills/ads-tiktok/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads microsoft`
Microsoft/Bing Ads deep analysis. Read `.agent/skills/ads-microsoft/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads creative`
Cross-platform creative quality audit. Read `.agent/skills/ads-creative/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads landing`
Landing page quality assessment. Read `.agent/skills/ads-landing/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads budget`
Budget allocation and bidding strategy review. Read `.agent/skills/ads-budget/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

### `/ads plan`
Strategic ad plan. Read `.agent/skills/ads-plan/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process. Load industry template from `.agent/skills/ads-plan/assets/` based on the user's business type.

### `/ads competitor`
Competitor ad intelligence. Read `.agent/skills/ads-competitor/SKILL.md` and follow its complete process.

## General Instructions

1. Always read the main orchestrator first: `.agent/skills/ads/SKILL.md`
2. Reference files are at `.agent/skills/ads/references/` — load on-demand as instructed by each sub-skill
3. Industry templates are at `.agent/skills/ads-plan/assets/`
4. Follow the scoring system from `ads/references/scoring-system.md` for health scores
5. Apply the 3x Kill Rule and Quality Gates from the main orchestrator
